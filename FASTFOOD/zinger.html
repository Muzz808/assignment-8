Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken fillet ( 4 to 5 )
  • Iceberg lettuce ( 4 to 5 leaves )
  • Eggs ( 4 )
  • Plain flour ( 1/2 cup )
  • Cornflour ( 1/2 cup )
  • Mustard powder ( 1 tsp )
  • Baking powder ( 1 tsp )
  • Chees slices ( 4 to 5 )
  • Hot sauce ( as required )
  • Mayonnaise ( as required )
  • Hot milk ( 1 cup )
  • Flour ( 1 tbsp )
  • Chicken cube ( 1 )
  • Sugar ( 1 tsp )
  • White pepper ( 1 tsp )
  • Salt ( to taste )
  • Buns ( 4 to 6 )
  • Oil ( as required )

Method

  3. Fry chicken till golden brown.
  4. Take round bun and cut in the middle, grease the pan lightly and toast both sides. Put hot milk and then mayonnaise on the buns.
