LEMON PEPPER FRIED FISH

Recepi

INGREDIENTS

  • Fish -For frying you can use any firm white fleshed fish. We love to use seer fish or paarai meen. Make sure you make deep slashes on the fish if you are frying it whole.
  • Spices -the basic combination of spice powders are chilli , coriander, turmeric, black pepper and garam masala.
  • Ginger & Garlic -freshly ground ginger and garlic is mainly for flavor and it helps to removes the fishy smell.
  • Lemon -the most essential ingredient is lemon juice or vinegar.
  • Curry Leaves -curry leaves is finely chopped and added in marination.
  • Coconut Oil -frying fish in coconut oil adds flavor and taste . If you haven't tried it so far, I strongly recommend you to give it a go.

HOW TO MAKE FISH FRY RECIPE (STEPWISE PICTURES)

  1. Take all your ingredients for marinating fish. You need chilli powder, turmeric, coriander, garam masala powder, black pepper powder, ginger garlic paste, curry leaves, coriander leaves, lemon juice and salt.
    I like to use a mix of kashmiri chilli powder and regular chilli powder. kashmiri chilli powder adds colour.
  2. Mix all the ingredients given for marination together. I like to use a plate or a shallow bowl for mixing the marination.
  3. Add in lemon juice. Sometimes I add vinegar to the marination. It adds a tangy taste to the marination which is essential for fried fish.
  4. Mix the marination together with your hands. Make sure the marination doesn't turn too runny. You want it to be thick.
  5. Now you can see the marination looks dry. You may add a splash of water in this and mix. Since this doesn't have fish added yet, you can taste and check for seasoning.
  6. Add some water or more lemon juice to your marination to adjust the consistency.
  7. Now the marination masala is ready. It has to be thick like paste.
  8. Take your fish pieces. Since this is a whole fish, make deep slashes on the fish. The marination will penetrate through these gashes. You can use any fish like king fish steaks, seer fish or even anchovies.
  9. Apply the marination all over the fish. Apply evenly into the slashes.
  10. Let the fish marinate for minimum 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  11. Make sure you apply the marination inside the fish as well.
